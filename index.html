Top Attractions in Dubai

From the tallest skyscrapers to traditional souks, Dubai offers an incredible array of attractions.

  • Burj Khalifa - The tallest building in the world
  • Dubai Mall - A shopper's paradise
  • Dubai Fountain - A mesmerizing water show
  • Desert Safari - Experience the Arabian desert

Things to Do

Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, Dubai has something for everyone:

  1. Explore the Palm Jumeirah
  2. Relax at Jumeirah Beach
  3. Shop at the Gold Souk
  4. Take a ride on the Dubai Creek

Must-Visit Neighborhoods

  • Downtown Dubai - Home of the Burj Khalifa
  • Dubai Marina - A waterfront lifestyle destination
  • Deira - The historical heart of Dubai
  • Jumeirah - A coastal residential area
